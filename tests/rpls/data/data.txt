He awoke from a dire sleep among the willows of the northern fair, twitters
abound of the dawning dewbirds flowing through his perking ears like a river
of crystaline beauty. His armaments had rested beside him through the dreary
nightum fall, now prepared for the drudgery of his modern day. Dragging the sun
across his dewed eyes, the unwarmed air of a northern climate stretched his
mobility and forced out man's routine morning roar.
